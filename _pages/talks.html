--- layout: single title: Conferences and presentations permalink: /conferences/ author_profile: true --- I use conferences as spaces to share work in progress, develop key research skills, and engage with the broader mathematics research education community. I am grateful for the opportunities I have had to present my work across Aotearoa New Zealand and internationally, and to learn from scholars whose work has shaped my own thinking. As a student researcher, conferences have played an important role in both presenting and developing my research, made possible through the support of my university and the Department of Mathematics. Below is a snapshot of my conference papers, presentations, and participation across local and international research communities.
